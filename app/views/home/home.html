Artist Bio

Higher Living Art

Hi, I'm Lisa Benson and I am excited to share my fun and inspiring creations with you. I currently live in Utah, and am an Actress/Model/Singer/Performer/Healer....and Artist!

Long ago, I remember watching a street artist paint with spray paint as our family was on vacation somewhere, and my parents decided to buy some when we got home so we could try it out ourselves!

​

We painted some really cool space scenes, with planets and stars, and whatever else we could think of.

This inspired me later in life, and I thought I'd try some new things with spray paint.... and boy has it been fun!

I have now ventured into fluid acrylics, and even mixing the two mediums together. Loving every minute of it!!

​

Creating with color has been so healing for me. It's given me a sense of freedom of expression, and it has really been exciting to see what new things I can come up with.

I am continuing to expand the kinds of art I create, and I am having such a blast! Anything is possible, right?!

If you are interested in purchasing any of my paintings, or would like to commission me to do a piece for you, please message me - I'd love to create something wonderful for you.

Enjoy!
